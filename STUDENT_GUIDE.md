The search portal allows you to find your documents using different methods:

### Search Methods

You can search using any of the following information:

1. **Roll Number** - Your student roll number (e.g., `2024001`)
2. **Registration Number** - Your registration number (e.g., `REG2024001`)
3. **Mobile Number** - Your registered mobile number (e.g., `1234567890`)
4. **Date of Birth** - Your date of birth (format: `YYYY-MM-DD` or `DD/MM/YYYY`)

### How to Search

1. **Go to the search page** at `/search`
2. **Enter your search information** in one of the search fields
3. **Click the "Search" button**
4. **View your results** - Your documents will appear if found

## âœ… Verifying Your Certificate

Certificates may include a QR code for verification purposes.

### Using the QR Code

1. **Scan the QR code** on your certificate using your smartphone camera or QR scanner app
2. **You'll be redirected** to a verification page
3. **View the verification details**:
   - Student name
   - Document details
   - Issue date
   - Authenticity status

### Manual Verification

If you have a verification code (hash):

1. Go to: `https://[your-university].educert.com/verify/[hash]`
2. Replace `[hash]` with your verification code
3. View the verification results

## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### "No Documents Found"

**Possible Reasons:**
- You entered incorrect information
- Your documents haven't been published yet by the university
- You're on the wrong university portal

**Solutions:**
- Double-check your roll number, registration number, or other details
- Try alternative search methods (mobile number, DOB)
- Contact your university if you believe documents should be available

### "Document Not Available"

**Possible Reasons:**
- Document is not yet published
- Document has been unpublished by administration

**Solution:**
- Contact your university administration for assistance

### QR Code Not Scanning

**Solutions:**
- Ensure good lighting when scanning
- Hold your phone steady
- Try a different QR scanner app
- Use manual verification with the hash code

### Download Not Working

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure your browser allows downloads
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache and try again

### PDF Won't Open

**Solutions:**
- Install a PDF reader (Adobe Acrobat Reader, PDF viewer)
- Update your PDF reader to the latest version
- Try opening in a different PDF application
